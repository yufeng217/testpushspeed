Map shot lets you share either the whole or a part of the map with someone not nearby in seconds. Here's how to:

Do Mapshot:

  1. Open a map.
  2. You have two manners to begin Mapshot:
    • Click 'F7'.
    • Choose 'Tools > Map Shot' on the menu.
  3. Select the area with mouse.
  4. Chosen area will be highlight.
  5. Change the area scope by dragging and moving the slider on the border.
  6. Double click the chosen area, there will be a dialog to let you save image file.*

Note:

  • The saved image file is JPG.
  • You can change Map shot settings via choosing "Edit > Preferences > Mind Map > Mapshot" on the menu.
